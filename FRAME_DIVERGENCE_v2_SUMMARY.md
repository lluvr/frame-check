## Abstract

Frame Divergence v2 is the integrated architecture for frame-divergence as a lifecycle of operations across a layered taxonomy. Frames are organized in five layers (L0 receiver state, L1 atomic axes, L2 simultaneous composites, L2.5 sequential chains, L3 reality construct). Operations are organized in a five-stage lifecycle (detection, divergence, chaining, convergence, grounding). Nine cross-cutting design principles bind the architecture, with catalog grounded-authorship as the primary empire moat (Principle P5) and L3.3 first-person enactive grounding as the strong falsifiable bet on the AGI-era anchor. The architecture is the first integrated proposal we know of for a layered, lifecycle-aware, citation-bound, MCP-distributable frame-divergence architecture. Seven falsifiable predictions (P1 through P7; P1-P2 carried from v1, P3-P7 added in v2) are pre-registered with bidirectional cross-references to five empirical claims (Claims A through E). The catalog discipline is operationalized across 19 active Frame Vocabulary Standard (FVS) entries, all retrofitted with grounded-authorship sections (authorship, context of testing, failure record, success record, lived-experience anchor, friction-cost estimate, applicability metadata, empirical track record). Honest scope: an information-processing model of perspective shift in pragmatic problem-solving and decision-making, with enactive elements at the receiver-state and act-instantiation layers; not an enactive theory of cognition, not a universal theory of frames, not a model of art-making, contemplative practice, worldview formation, or grief work.

## 1. Introduction: the AGI-era discernment thesis

Retrieval and synthesis compound. Current LLMs pull hundreds of sources, traverse citation graphs, and produce grounded answers with decreasing hallucination rates. That trajectory is not reversing. Grounding as a value proposition commoditizes on the timeline of the next two model generations.

What does not compound on the same curve is discernment. Three observations carry the argument:

**Simulation is not discrimination.** An LLM can simulate named critical theorists, role-play framings, and generate "have you considered X" lists. What it cannot do without a catalog calibrated to the thinker's domain and the document's evidence is tell the thinker WHICH absent frames matter for THIS decision. Generic simulation at catalog scale is noise. Catalog-calibrated divergence with domain-relevance filtering is signal.

**Synthesis-easy makes discernment-hard.** In an era where any answer is generable, the scarce skill is noticing what was not asked. Divergence is a discernment tool, not a synthesis tool. Its value grows as synthesis commoditizes. The economic argument inverts: the more powerful LLMs become at generating plausible content, the more valuable it becomes to have an auditable instrument that flags what the generation did not consider.

**The structural invariant holds through AGI.** Humans reason through frames. Any moment of thinking has frames taken, frames rejected, and frames invisible. Making the invisible visible against a named catalog, with citations, is catalog-anchored structural measurement of what is absent. It is not synthesis. Better synthesis models do not make this operation easier; they make demand for it higher.

Frame Divergence v1 (`FRAME_DIVERGENCE_v1.md`) committed to a narrow operational definition: catalog-minus-present. Surface FVS entries the document does not exhibit, citation-bound, faithfulness-guaranteed, non-prescriptive, bounded by the catalog. v2 absorbs that narrow operation as one species within a wider structural and operational architecture.

## 2. Two views of the architecture

v2 is structured along two orthogonal axes.

**View 1 (structural): the frame taxonomy.** Five layers describing what kinds of frames exist.

**View 2 (operational): the lifecycle.** Five stages describing what is done with frames.

The intersection: operations apply across multiple layers; layers feed operations. Detection finds L1 atomic shifts and L2 composites in a document. Divergence surfaces absences from any layer. Chaining composes L1 atomic shifts and L2 composites in sequence. Convergence selects from outputs at any layer using criteria-frames (themselves L1 VALUE-WHY shifts plus catalog entries). Grounding crosses from cognitive layers into L3.3 act-instantiation.

The lifecycle loops: grounding outcomes update detection (current state changes); detection feeds new divergence; new divergence informs chaining; chaining produces new convergence; convergence produces new acts. The unit of value is the loop, not any single stage.

## 3. The five-layer taxonomy

### 3.1 Layer 0: Receiver State

Layer 0 is the precondition layer. Receiver state determines which frames are accessible to the receiver before any L1+ operation can fire. Frames not accessible to current receiver state cannot be received cognitively even when they exist in the catalog. v2 specifies six operational dimensions (engagement state, direction of motion, trigger vs sustained, conditioning state, identity coupling, optimization function) shared across receiver types (human, AI, hybrid), with mechanism notes per receiver type. The structural human-AI asymmetry on identity-detachment is named as load-bearing: AI receivers default to identity-detached, human receivers default to identity-attached, and architecture work leverages this complementarity.

### 3.2 Layer 1: Atomic Shifts (Axes)

L1 is the layer of single-axis perspective shifts. Eight axes are named: VANTAGE-WHO (observer/actor identity), VANTAGE-WHEN (temporal position), VANTAGE-WHERE (spatial), SALIENCE-WHAT (foreground; domain analogy), GRAMMAR-HOW (structural decomposition), VALUE-WHY (what matters; on what scale), METHOD (epistemology), CONSTRAINT (what is variable). Three additional modes are not continuous-axis shifts: RHETORICAL/CONTENT (named patterns in prose; the existing FVS slice), SERENDIPITY (unintended frame-break recognition), META-FRAMING (announcing or shifting context-envelope per Bateson). Axes are non-orthogonal; cross-domain universality is unverified.

### 3.3 Layer 2: Simultaneous Composites

L2 constructs combine multiple L1 axes at one moment in time. The level current LLMs handle fluently because training data is dense in the corresponding forms (narrative, scenario, dialogue, hypothesis-chain). Initial candidate catalog includes scenario-story, counterfactual hypothesis chain, persona-in-history, constraint cascade, multi-stakeholder dialogue, domain-translation narrative, genealogy frame, system-nesting shift, edge-case generator, steelman-strawman pair.

### 3.4 Layer 2.5: Frame Chains

L2.5 is the layer of ordered frame sequences where each frame consumes the previous's output. Chains are the operationalization of brainstorming-shaped activities. Chain anatomy: input, sequence, flow logic, accumulator, convergence step, grounding step. Initial chain library includes adversarial chain (optimist > realist > adversary > premortem > mitigation > commitment), domain-transfer chain, stakeholder chain, time-horizon chain, decomposition chain, constraint-cascade chain, brainstorm chain, genealogy chain. Chains are the primary MCP-distributable operation.

### 3.5 Layer 3: Reality Construct

L3 is the layer where exploration becomes constitutive rather than analytic. Three sub-modes: L3.1 ontology shift (Kuhnian paradigm; adjacent territory acknowledged but not claimed), L3.2 constitutive recognition (constructivism; adjacent), L3.3 act-instantiation (enactive or pragmatist). v2 claims L3.3 as the strong falsifiable bet on the AGI-era empire anchor: as AI capabilities scale, L1 simulation and L2 composition commoditize; L3.3 stays irreducibly human (or human-coupled-to-reality) because it requires first-person enactive grounding that pure cognition without first-person agency cannot replicate. The load-bearing claim is about agency-with-consequence, not biology per se; embodiment is the current human mechanism for it.

## 4. The five-stage lifecycle

The lifecycle stages: **detection** (frames present in current text or thinking), **divergence** (frames absent, surfaced from catalog), **chaining** (sequential composition of frames), **convergence** (criteria-based commitment from divergent or chained outputs), **grounding** (act plus reality returns confirmation or disconfirmation; updates frame validity).

Convergence is elevated to a first-class lifecycle stage in v2. Convergence anatomy: input from divergence and/or chain operations; criteria as themselves frames (typically VALUE-WHY axis activations, surfaced explicitly in all cases); decision rule (multi-attribute weighting, satisficing threshold, lexicographic ordering, max-expected-utility); commitment graduation (full commit, hypothesis-with-revision-points, parallel paths, deferred-commit-with-monitoring); disagreement handling (conflicts preserved not collapsed). Even AGI converges; capability does not remove the necessity of choosing among alternatives.

Grounding closes the loop. The actor commits, acts, and reality returns confirmation or disconfirmation. Without grounding, convergence is theoretical. Grounding outcomes update frame validity over time; the catalog's empirical track record is the accumulation of grounding outcomes.

## 5. Nine cross-cutting design principles

P1 Divergence serves convergence (the cycle is the unit of value, not any single stage).

P2 Frames open questions; questions create friction; friction creates novelty (question-friction is the primary value measurement).

P3 Layers have causal loops, not strict hierarchy (L3.3 → L0 retroactive; L0 → L1+ gating).

P4 Reflection disrupts practice (substrate is rich; surface must be invisible at the moment of use).

**P5 Catalog requires grounded authorship, not just citation.** Each catalog entry carries the public-facing fields: authorship (named, dated), context of testing, failure record, success record, lived-experience anchor, applicability metadata, empirical track record when available. Catalog entries that combine named authorship with grounded operational context provide citation-anchored discipline that scales beyond template-only references. **This is the primary empire moat regardless of AI capability trajectory.**

P6 Construct-honesty extends to construct-safety (forbidden-operations register, divergence-theater detection, convergence-honesty discipline, L0-state-respecting language).

P7 Three-audience surface design (AI agents, flex-humans, survival humans).

P8 Honest scope discipline (every claim names its evidence basis; falsifiable predictions registered; strong bets named as strong bets, not facts).

P9 Structural human-AI asymmetry on identity-detachment. Identity-attachment is the typical baseline tendency in human receivers; identity-detachment is cultivated through deliberate practice. AI receivers have a different identity-baseline (training-distribution plus system frame plus RLHF disposition); the architecture-mode register is more readily accessible in AI receivers than in untrained human receivers. Architecture work leverages this complementarity. Load-bearing across §3.4 receiver state, §12 surface design, §17.4 empire-moat positioning.

## 6. Catalog discipline and retrofit milestone

The Frame Vocabulary Standard (FVS) catalog is the operational instantiation of P5. As of 2026-04-25, all 19 active FVS entries (FVS-001 through FVS-019; FVS-020 retired per `FRAME_DIVERGENCE_v1.md` §5.1.4) carry the v2 §11 grounded-authorship section.

Each retrofit applies seven public-facing fields: authorship dated, context of testing (corpora, case studies, dates), failure record (typically three specific failure modes with empirical anchors), success record (typically two operationalized cases), lived-experience anchor (held open per honest-scope discipline pending curator authorship), applicability metadata (domains, decision types, stake levels, inappropriate contexts), empirical track record (consolidated cross-family reliability plus case-study outcomes plus integration points). Plus an internal-operational field (friction-cost estimate) that supports curation-layer workflow but does not appear in public-facing entry summaries or citation contexts.

The catalog moat is now structurally disciplined across all 19 active entries. A reader encountering any FVS entry sees the same structure: original v1 content (identification, decision-readiness implication, generation affordances, worked examples, branch applicability, vocabulary connections, cross-family reliability) plus v2 §11 grounded-authorship section. Phase 1C-revised entries (FVS-012 Uncertainty Frame, FVS-016 Authority by Citation, FVS-018 Scope Narrowing) include explicit pre-revision-vs-post-revision documentation in the failure record, demonstrating principled revision discipline rather than moving-target definitions.

**Structural-grade vs full-grade distinction.** As of 2026-04-25, the 19 retrofitted entries are at v2 §11 STRUCTURAL grade: all 8 fields are present and operationally substantive. The lived-experience anchor field is uniformly held "Open" with entry-specific criteria per honest-scope discipline, pending curator authorship. Full v2 §11 grade requires lived-experience anchors authored per entry. Curator authorship is maintainer-side activation work; anchor production methodology is operator-execution-pending. The catalog moat at structural-grade is materially stronger than v1's citation-only discipline; full-grade is the next milestone.

## 8. Empire-moat positioning and falsifiable predictions

The empire is the catalog with grounded authorship (Principle P5 in §10.5; primary moat) plus L3.3 first-person enactive grounding (Prediction P5 in §14.2; strong falsifiable bet). The first lasts regardless of AI trajectory; the second is the trajectory-contingent additional bet.

Prediction P6 in §14.2 (tested by §15.2 Claim B) is the prediction whose falsification would compress the catalog moat to citation-only. P6 passing is therefore necessary for Principle P5 in §10.5 to hold at full strength; P6 is the most consequential single empirical test for the empire claim.

Both anchors ship together. If Prediction P5 (L3.3) falsifies, the catalog moat alone sustains the position. If P6 falsifies, the catalog moat itself compresses and the empire claim weakens substantially.

Falsifiable predictions registered (with horizons):

- **P1** (24-month from v1 ratification, 2028-04-23). At least one publicly authored frame catalog or divergence-style capability ships from a third party.
- **P2** (12-month from MCP publish + parts 2-4 completion). At least one agent framework or MCP-consuming product integrates frame divergence with citations to FVS entries.
- **P3** (12-month from chain-tool ship). Chain-output beats single-frame-output on decision quality (test: §15.1 Claim A).
- **P4** (24-month from L0-aware language ship). L0-aware language correlates with adoption and trust (test: §15.4 Claim D).
- **P5** (60-month from spec ratification, 2031-04-25). L3.3-coupled-to-authored-catalog survives AGI capability scaling (test: §15.5 Claim E).
- **P6** (12-month from web/SaaS launch). Catalog with grounded authorship beats catalog with citation-only on user trust and adoption (test: §15.2 Claim B).
- **P7** (24-month from instrumentation deployment). Question-friction generated correlates with novelty in downstream action (test: §15.3 Claim C).

## 9. Honest scope and limitations

v2 is an information-processing model of perspective shift in pragmatic problem-solving and decision-making, with enactive elements at L0 receiver state and L3.3 act-instantiation. It is not an enactive theory of cognition, not a universal theory of frames, not a model of art-making, contemplative practice, worldview formation, or grief work. Cross-domain universality is unverified and is not claimed.

Frame-as-term carries multiple senses across cognitive science (Goffman interaction frame, Lakoff conceptual frame, Bateson meta-communicative envelope, Schank-Abelson script, Minsky knowledge structure, Fillmore frame semantics). v2 treats frame primarily as frame-as-catalog-entry when discussing the existing FVS catalog, and as frame-as-perspective-shift-vehicle when discussing L1 axes, L2 composites, and L2.5 chains.

Within the named scope, v2 is the first integrated proposal we know of for a layered, lifecycle-aware, citation-bound, MCP-distributable frame-divergence architecture. Adjacent literature exists (TRIZ, lateral thinking, six thinking hats, premortem, oblique strategies, scenario planning, IBIS, decision quality, red teaming, role-storming, Koestler bisociation, Schank-Abelson scripts) and informs the work without being subsumed by it.

L0 is doctrine, not validated layer-with-detected-structure. Dimensions named are an initial falsifiable proposal; whether they are genuinely orthogonal sub-axes or facets of one underlying state is unproven from current data. Cross-curator validation is the next test.

The 19 retrofitted catalog entries are at v2 §11 structural grade; lived-experience anchors are held open per honest-scope discipline pending curator authorship. The catalog moat is strengthened by the structural retrofit; full v2 §11 conformance (including authored anchors) is open work.
